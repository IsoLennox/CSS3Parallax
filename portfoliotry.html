About Isobel Lennox

You've found me! My name is Isobel Lennox. I am a perpetually growing developer based in Vancouver, Washington. I have a wonderful little family including my husband Chris and my daughter, Zora. I am currently attending Clark College for my AAT in Web Development and CS in Programming.
